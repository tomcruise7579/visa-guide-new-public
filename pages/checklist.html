Pro Tips for Your Application

  • ✓ Start Early: Don't wait until the last minute. Begin gathering documents 2-3 months before your travel date.
  • ✓ Make Copies: Keep copies of all documents. Originals might not be returned immediately.
  • ✓ Verify Requirements: Check the specific requirements with the embassy or consulate for your country.
  • ✓ Original Documents: Ensure all documents are authentic and properly certified where required.
  • ✓ Follow Instructions: Strictly follow the embassy's instructions. Missing items can delay processing.
